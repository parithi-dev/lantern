Features

Everything you need to
understand your network

Built for home networks. Runs on a Raspberry Pi, old laptop, or your main machine.

Real-Time Discovery

Automatically detects every device on your network. See new devices the moment they connect.

Live Dashboard

Beautiful dark-themed dashboard with real-time latency charts, bandwidth graphs, and device status.

Smart Alerts

Get notified when new devices join or when latency spikes. Know what's happening on your network.

Bandwidth Tracking

Monitor download and upload speeds over time. See peak usage and identify bandwidth hogs.

Historical Data

latency, device presence, and bandwidth history stored locally. No cloud dependency.

Zero Configuration

Single command setup. Python agent + Angular UI. Runs on Linux, macOS, and Windows.

Showcase

Mission Control for
your home network

A dark, immersive interface that makes your network data feel alive.

Device Tracking

Every device on your network displayed in a clean card grid. See hostname, IP, MAC, vendor, and online status at a glance.

  • Vendor identification via MAC OUI lookup
  • First seen and last seen timestamps
  • Device detail panel with latency chart
  • Sort and filter your devices
